Main Courses :

Couscous

A traditional North African dish of steamed semolina wheat, often served with a stew of meat (lamb or chicken), vegetables, and chickpeas.

Tajine

A slow-cooked stew made with various ingredients such as lamb, chicken, or beef, combined with fruits like apricots or prunes, and spices like saffron and cinnamon.

Mechoui

A whole lamb, marinated with spices and roasted until tender and crispy. It's often served at large gatherings and special occasions.

Desserts :

Makroute

Semolina-based pastries filled with dates or nuts, shaped into diamonds or rectangles, and fried until crispy. They are then soaked in honey or syrup.

Kalb el Louz

A traditional Algerian almond cake made from semolina, almonds, and flavored with orange blossom water, then baked and cut into diamond-shaped pieces.

Zgougou (or Zgougou Zehya)

A dessert made from roasted pine nuts mixed with a sweet, thick syrup.
